introduction

hi, my name is Idan Shtivi and I am 24 years old. I live in Holon with my perfect wife Sahar Mizrahi on Shoshana-Damari street. I have a big, happy, beautiful family, 3 sisters, happily married parents and two dogs.
When I was a kid I always got into some trouble.
Not studying not in classes the corridor was the best friend.
After years of failing my final certificates, my parents gave up, the teachers gave up and me too, I was discouraged. And a second before I was kicked out of school They decided to put me in a project called Turnaround
This project is taking kids that the system intended to throw out.
With over 7 out of 8 failing a final certificate.
And there they taught me a very important thing

Self-learning - teach me how to learn
From the child who fails to pass one test and almost flew out of school, I became a school graduate with 12 years of full matriculation and when no one believed in me, I believed
Today I can tell about myself that I am a curious person who likes to learn something new every day, the areas I have touched so far in my life are a little far from each other but you can always find the connection between them.
